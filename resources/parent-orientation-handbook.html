About Girl Scouts

Girl Scouts builds girls of courage, confidence, and character who make the world a better place. Through hands-on activities, outdoor adventures, and working together, girls learn important life skills while having fun!

The Girl Scout Promise:

On my honor, I will try:
To serve God and my country,
To help people at all times,
And to live by the Girl Scout Law.

The Girl Scout Law:

I will do my best to be honest and fair, friendly and helpful, considerate and caring, courageous and strong, and responsible for what I say and do, and to respect myself and others, respect authority, use resources wisely, make the world a better place, and be a sister to every Girl Scout.

Our Troop Vision

Our troop will focus on:

  • Adventure - Getting outside, trying new things, exploring
  • Science & Problem-Solving - Hands-on experiments and challenges
  • Practical Skills - Learning things they can actually use
  • Confidence Building - Supporting each girl to try, fail, and succeed
  • Fun! - Because that's what being 6-7 is all about

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my daughter can't make a meeting?

Just let me know! We understand life happens. If she misses badge work, we can catch her up.

Do I have to volunteer?

We do need parent help to run meetings safely and successfully. Even if you can't attend every meeting, there are many ways to help!

What if we can't afford the uniform?

Don't let cost be a barrier! Talk to me privately. Girl Scouts has resources to help.

Can siblings come to meetings?

Generally, no - this is special time for our Daisies. If you need to bring a sibling occasionally, please ask ahead of time.

What if my daughter wants to quit?

Talk to me first! Sometimes girls just need a little encouragement or a different role in activities. We want every girl to feel included.
