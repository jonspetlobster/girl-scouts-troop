Parent Orientation Meeting - Agenda

Megan's Reference Guide

Total Meeting Time: ~60 minutes

1. Welcome & Introductions (5 min)

  • Introduce yourself
  • Quick round: each parent shares their name and daughter's name
  • Thank them for signing up!

2. What is Girl Scouts? (8 min)

  • Brief history/mission: building girls of courage, confidence, character
  • The Girl Scout Promise and Law (don't worry about memorizing yet!)
  • Age levels: Daisies (K-1st), Brownies, Juniors, etc.
  • Girl-led, hands-on learning

3. Our Troop Vision (7 min)

What makes our troop special:

  • More adventure and outdoor activities
  • Science experiments and problem-solving
  • Hands-on skills the girls can actually use
  • Building confidence through challenges
  • Fun, supportive environment

4. Meeting Details (5 min)

  • When: [Day/time]
  • Where: [Location]
  • How often: [Weekly/bi-weekly]
  • What to bring: Water bottle, any materials requested ahead of time
  • Drop-off/pick-up: [Procedures and timing]

5. What We'll Be Doing (5 min)

  • Earning Daisy petals (building our flower!)
  • Badge work (3-4 badges this season)
  • Field trips and special activities
  • Service project
  • End-of-year bridging ceremony

6. Costs & Financials (5 min)

  • Registration fee: Already paid ✓
  • Uniform: ~$70 (tunic, sash) - OPTIONAL for this short season (but we're earning badges/petals so girls will be excited!)
  • Troop dues: [Your amount if any, or say "none"]
  • Field trips/special activities: Approximately [range]
  • Cookie season: [When], great fundraiser for troop activities
  • What troop funds will cover

7. Volunteer Needs (7 min)

We need help to make this work!

  • At least 1-2 parent helpers at each meeting (safety requirement)
  • Field trip chaperones
  • Snack rotation schedule (pass around sign-up sheet)
  • Special skills? Share crafts, hobbies, careers with the girls!
  • Behind-the-scenes help (coordinating activities, paperwork)

Background checks: Required for regular volunteers (I'll send info)

8. Safety & Important Info (5 min)

  • Need emergency contact forms from everyone (hand out if not already collected)
  • Allergies: Please share any food allergies NOW (pass around sheet)
  • Behavior expectations: respectful, inclusive, supportive
  • Communication: How I'll reach you [group text/email/app]

9. How Parents Can Support (3 min)

  • Volunteering at meetings or behind the scenes
  • Helping with badge activities at home
  • Sharing ideas and feedback
  • Being flexible as we learn together!

10. Q&A (10 min)

Open floor for questions

11. Wrap-Up

  • Pass out parent handout
  • Collect emergency forms and allergy info
  • Next meeting: [date/time]
  • Thank you!

Remember: You've got this! Be yourself and let your enthusiasm shine! 🌟
