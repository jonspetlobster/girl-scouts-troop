Tips for Maximizing Progress

  1. Layer, don't stack — a nature walk can count toward a petal AND a badge
  2. Ceremony matters — call each girl's name, everyone claps when she gets a petal
  3. Award as earned — don't save all petals for a big ceremony; instant gratification works at this age
  4. Use VTK plans — don't reinvent the wheel
  5. The last meeting is sacred — make the celebration memorable for families
  6. Photograph everything — parents love it
  7. Missed a petal meeting? — 5-minute chat at next meeting counts
